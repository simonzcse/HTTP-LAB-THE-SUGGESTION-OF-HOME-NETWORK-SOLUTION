WiFi is a wireless technology that connects devices to the internet without any physical wired connection. Introduced in 1999, it is the most popular type of connection used today.

Introduced in 1973, Ethernet is a way of connecting devices in a LAN. Consisting of an Ethernet cable, hub, crossover cable and router, it enables devices to transmit data over a network.
